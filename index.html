🍓 Fruit Match Game 🍍

👧 You: 0 🤖 Robot: 0

🍉 How to Play

  • Each card has 5 fruits — 4 in the corners, 1 in the center.
  • There is one center card shared between both players.
  • You and the robot each get your own card.
  • Whoever sees the matching fruit between your card and the center card first, clicks it!
  • The winner places their card on top of the center pile and gets a new card.
  • The game ends when the deck runs out.
  • The player with the most correct matches wins!

🧠 Tip: You can adjust the robot's speed using the dropdown above.

🏁 When you're ready, press "Start Game" to begin!
